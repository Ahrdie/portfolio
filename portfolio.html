Virtual Reality CNC workshop

Summer term 2017

About

The virtual reality application was created by me during my obligatory internship at the medium-sized company CNC Keller as a prototype.
Its focus was on the interaction with Objects in the scene; especially with the virtual CNC-milling machine.

Exemplary elements of the application

  • CNC-milling machine that was partly operable through control panel
  • Information system for learning content
  • Custom interactive part-highlighter, that exposes integral parts of the milling machine and turns the remaining parts transparent
